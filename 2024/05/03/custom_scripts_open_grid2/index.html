Open stop order in both directions

3. Two-way stop order:
Scripts\_OrderGridOpenP\OpenPairsStopOrder.mq5

Application scenario: Applicable to large unilateral markets.
Scenario 1: Place an order 10 seconds before the market starts
Take Profit & Stop Loss: Stop Loss +1.5, Take Profit +3. (Automatically adjust scripts as needed)
Function description: Batch billing within the range of 0 ~ 2 or 2 ~ 4 or 4 ~ 6.
Function 1: Click the first time, open orders from 0.4 + 0 ~ 2 in the upper and lower ranges, and open orders at intervals of 0.2. The denser the better.
Function 2: Click the second time, open an order from the upper and lower range of 0.4 + 2 ~ 4, and open an order at an interval of 0.2.
Function 3: Click the 3rd time, open orders from the upper and lower range of 0.4 + 4 ~ 6, and open orders at intervals of 0.2.
Function 4: Tap the 4th time, and so on.
Position Description: The total position will become larger when you click on it multiple times. Refer to another article on modifying positions.
Note 1: The risk of 2 points is high and you may lose money, and you will not be more if you are not sure.
