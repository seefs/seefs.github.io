Limit to open a position

1. Open a position from the current price grid: Open a position from the current price flat up or down.
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen\OpenBuyLimitOrder.mq5
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen\OpenSellLimitOrder.mq5

Application scenario: Place an order at a lower cost grid at the current price.
Scenario 1: At the beginning of an uptrend, the pullback opens long, I want a lower price, but it appears for a short time.
Scenario 2: Seize some unexpected opportunities, such as a large pullback in the rally.
Scenario 3: At the end of the market, guess the bottom and guess the top, maybe it is better to place an order on the grid.
Scenario 4: Quotes that often appear to reverse in battle.

2. Open a position at a specified price grid: Open a position flat up or down at the specified price.
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen4\OpenBuyLimitOrder_4In2.mq5
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen4\OpenBuyLimitOrder_4Out2.mq5
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen4\OpenSellLimitOrder_4In2.mq5
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen4\OpenSellLimitOrder_4Out2.mq5

Scenario: Place an order at an estimated cost.
Scenario 1: This was originally done to consider the Limit+Stop combination. If you consider a callback when you are bullish, open a long order with Limit within -$4, and open a long order with Stop above +$2
Scenario 2: Limit should be used in volatile markets. If you use stop, you will lose money.
Scenario 3: Key position reversal.
Tiling range: Each tile, within the $2 price range. The range can be expanded multiple times.
(After many clicks, the test found that the price range has not changed, and it will be revised next time)

Long Position Function: Roll up or down from -$4 to open a long position. Click again to tile from -$6 or -$2.
Short position: Open a short position from +$4 upwards or downwards. Click again to tile from +$6 or +$2.
Position: The position of each operation is about 1%, please see the parameters of line 13, you can increase the recompilation.
Take Profit and Stop Loss: Now I don’t know if there is a good outside with Take Profit and Stop Loss.

Stop to open a position

3. Open a position from the current price grid: Open a position from the current price flat up or down.
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen\OpenBuyStopOrder.mq5
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen\OpenSellStopOrder.mq5

Application scenario: omitted.

4. Open a position at a specified price grid: Open a position flat up or down at the specified price.
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen4\OpenBuyStopOrder_4In2.mq5
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen4\OpenBuyStopOrder_4Out2.mq5
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen4\OpenSellStopOrder_4In2.mq5
Scripts\_OrderGridOpen4\OpenSellStopOrder_4Out2.mq5

Application scenario: Try not to use small quotes, and try not to use low heat quotes.
Scenario 1: Major news market, orders can be opened within the last 30 seconds. But consider how big the range of fluctuations is.
Scenario 2: Retail investors concentrate their chips and quickly open reverse risk orders.
Scenario 3: The bearish reversal will be relatively large, and the volatility will be relatively large, and a risk order will be opened.
Scenario 4: Unilateral market, or trend, or the market that is manipulated by institutions to wash up and down, fluctuates greatly after the breakout.
Scenario 5: It is estimated that it will pull up and down, and only open orders other than plus or minus $4.
Tiling range: Each tile, within the $2 price range. The range can be expanded multiple times.
Long Position Function: Roll up or down from +$4 to open a long position. Click again to tile from +$6 or +$2.
Short position function: Open a short position from -$4 upwards or downwards. Click again to tile from -$6 or -$2.
Take Profit and Stop Loss: Stop loss must be brought. If there is a take profit position, it can be increased. Trying to take profit will cause slippage (in your favor).
